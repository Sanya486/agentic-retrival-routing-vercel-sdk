# Linking your Scrimba account to Discord

Join our Discord at [scrimba.com/discord](http://scrimba.com/discord)

Our Discord server is a thriving hub of students, industry professionals, and the Scrimba team, all coming together to learn, share, and connect.

Here’s how to link your Discord account to Scrimba and unlock the exclusive **Pro role** if you’re a Pro Member:

- First, click on the dropdown at the top left of our server

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580f76c003e400f646205e/file-Q4PDjkEqzR.png)

- Then in the dropdown menu, click on Linked Roles:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580f80a60dde1d5482c1fe/file-eA0clIwzrP.png)

- Then choose the role to link:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580f8d6101c17cbab8526e/file-1iKjVkFjyD.png)

- Then click Scrimba V2:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580f98a60dde1d5482c1ff/file-mhbG2cdnOP.png)

- A new browser window should pop up. Click **Authorize**:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580fa26101c17cbab8526f/file-2TQ0m1Z8jH.png)

- Then, back in discord, it should say that Scrimba V2 has connected. Click done:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580fb3c83f0231bca46d66/file-o4ZaaUrL7K.png)

- Then click Finish:

![](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/639fdb80a6780a675f7e8d40/images/67580fc46101c17cbab85270/file-yclpOhvLQe.png)

And you're good to go! Any issues please reach out to us.
