# How to join the Scrimba Discord

To access our Scrimba Discord, please use this link here [**https://scrimba.com/discord**](https://scrimba.com/discord) **.**

Subscribers get access to premium channels where they can ask for code reviews, get assistance with their work and more. If you're a subscriber please check out our article on how to enable you Pro Discord status here: [https://scrimba.helpscoutdocs.com/article/53-linking-your-scrimba-account-to-discord](https://scrimba.helpscoutdocs.com/article/53-linking-your-scrimba-account-to-discord)
